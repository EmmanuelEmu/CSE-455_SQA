.. container:: add-update-teacher-form Form for adding or updating a teacher's information. This template extends the ``main.html`` template and is used to display a form for adding or updating a teacher's information. It includes fields for the teacher's name, registration number, rank, department, email, phone, and description. The form allows users to submit the information for adding or updating a teacher.
